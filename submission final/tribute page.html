GAURAV TANEJA

aka The Flying Beast
9 july 1986-present

Initial life and education:


Gaurav Taneja, widely known as "Flying Beast," is a multifaceted personality in India,
recognized for his contributions as a pilot, bodybuilder, and popular YouTuber. He was born on July 9, 1986, in Kanpur,
Uttar Pradesh, India. He pursued his passion for flying by enrolling in the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur,
where he graduated with a degree in Civil Engineering. He has flown for major airlines, including IndiGo and AirAsia,
accumulating thousands of flying hours. He has also pursued Law from Delhi university.



Career and Family:


However, Gaurav's career took a significant turn when he decided to share his life and experiences on YouTube.
He started his YouTube channel, "Flying Beast," which initially focused on his life as a pilot and travel vlogs.
Over time, the channel evolved to include fitness tips, family vlogs, and lifestyle content. His engaging personality
and relatable content quickly attracted a large audience, making him one of India's most popular YouTubers.
In addition to "Flying Beast," he runs two other successful YouTube channels: "Fit Muscle TV," dedicated to fitness
and bodybuilding, and "Rasbhari Ke Papa," a family vlog channel. Gaurav is married to Ritu Rathee, who is also a commercial pilot.
They have two daughters, Kaira and Chaitravi, who frequently appear in their family vlogs, endearing the family to their audience.



Currently:


As of now, gaurav continues content creation as well as social media influence. He has also started two brands of his own
namely Rosier foods, which focuses mainly on organic food products, and Beast life, which provides premium quality gym
supplements. He is the best example of a person possessing versatility and consistency as balancing so many roles alomgside being
the best son,husband and father definitely isn't a doodle.
